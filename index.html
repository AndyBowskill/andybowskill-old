Non League

The best way for the English non league football fan to stay up to date with the fixtures, results, league tables and news from the twelve divisions that comprise the top four steps of the English National League System hierarchy.

Fixtures, results and league tables for the following divisions:

  • Step One: Football Conference National.
  • Step Two: Football Conference North, Football Conference South.
  • Step Three: Northern Premier League Premier Division, Southern League Premier Division, Isthmian League Premier Division.
  • Step Four: Northern Premier League Division One North, Northern Premier League Division One South, Southern League Division One Central, Southern League Division One South & West, Isthmian League Division One North, Isthmian League Division One South.

Scores are always up to date, even for games that are in-progress. Numerous news feeds/sources to keep you up to date with the latest developments in the non league football world.


Make-Up Kit

Produced in conjunction with a professional make-up artist, Make-Up Kit provides a clean and simple method of maintaining a categorised inventory of your make-up kit so that its details are always close to hand.

  • 19 make-up categories, each allowing unlimited make-up items to be added.

Each make-up item can:

  • Have its own image assigned, either taken using the device's camera or from the device's stored photos.
  • Have its own specific colour and shade selected to aid identification.
  • Have an estimated expiry date calculated according to its make-up category and date the item was first opened.
  • Show a preview of either its image or colour in the list of make-up items for a category.

UK Sale Calc

UK Sale Calc is the app to rely on when you need to quickly and easily calculate the sale price of that bargain item, and how much you will save compared to the original price.

Original prices can also be entered net of VAT (Value Added Tax) and a VAT percent can be specified and applied to the calculation if desired.
